1. Self Driving Car

I built an autonomous RC car using an Arduino R3 as the microcontroller.
The car is equipped with a Radar for object detection,
a single-point LiDAR for precise distance measurement, and a Webcam for visual input.
I utilized OpenCV for computer vision tasks, such as object and lane detection.
After completing the project, I modified the car into a drift RC car by adding tape to the rear tires for fun.
